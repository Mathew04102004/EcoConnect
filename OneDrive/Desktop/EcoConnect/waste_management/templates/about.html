About EcoConnect

At EcoConnect, we are committed to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future by revolutionizing waste management. Our platform provides an easy and efficient way for individuals and communities to schedule waste pickups, track their disposal process, and contribute to a greener planet.

🌍 Our Mission

We aim to reduce waste pollution by connecting households, businesses, and waste collectors through a seamless and eco-friendly digital platform. Our goal is to promote responsible waste disposal while encouraging recycling and sustainability initiatives.

🔹 Why Choose EcoConnect?

  • ✅ Easy Scheduling: Book waste pickups with just a few clicks.
  • ✅ Real-Time Tracking: Stay updated on your waste collection status.
  • ✅ Eco-Friendly Approach: Encourage recycling and responsible disposal.
  • ✅ Community Impact: Join hands with local organizations for a greener tomorrow.
